Contents of this folder:
* Fritzing - source files for the wiring schematics
* SimInvent_PCBs - The new PCBs by @PeteDD
* Legacy - The older Gagagu v2.0 PCBs. These are compatible, but please see the Wiki for details covering the special fixes made.
* PCB_pictures - Rendered PCB images. Note, additional images can also be viewed in the [Wiki Electronics section](https://github.com/barsk/Arduino_FFB_Yoke/wiki/Electronics)
* Wiring_diagram -  Diagram of the overall wiring
